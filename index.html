⚔️ Basics

  • You and your opponent fight on a 4×4 grid — you're on the bottom row, they're on top.
  • Cards auto-draw into 3 slots: Z (attacks/equipment), X (summons), C (items).
  • Win by reaching the loyalty limit or reducing the enemy's HP to 0.
  • Earn loyalty by defeating enemy summons.

🃏 Card Types

  • Attacks — Fire projectiles or melee strikes. Stronger attacks have longer draw cooldowns.
  • Summons — Allied creatures placed on the grid that fight automatically.
  • Items — Heals, buffs, shields, oils, food, and more. Stronger items take longer to draw the next card.
  • Equipment — Multi-use items in Z or C slot. Weaker equipment fires faster.

🌟 Elements

  • Every card and ninja has an element. Attacks deal 1.5× against weak elements, 0.5× against strong.
  • Elements also have on-hit effects: 🔥 burn, ⚡ stun, 🌿 lifesteal, ❄️ freeze, 🌑 poison, 🌪️ pushback, 💧 cleanse, 🪨 bubble shield.

💡 Tips

  • Dodge with Smoke Bomb — next incoming hit misses entirely.
  • Bubble shields block ALL damage for a duration — time them against big attacks.
  • Oils imbue your attacks with an element for 8s — use them to exploit weaknesses.
  • Speed scrolls make all your cooldowns tick 2× faster.
  • Food items give mixed stat boosts — HP, ATK, speed, or max HP.
  • Transformation items (ultra rare boss drops) change your element by transforming you and boost your max HP.
  • Build your deck in the Deck Editor — each slot (Z/X/C) needs 10 cards.
  • Defeat all enemies in a chapter to unlock the boss, then beat the boss for the next chapter.
